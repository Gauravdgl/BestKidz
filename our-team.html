MS. NEGAR KHODABANDI

Nursery Manager

Ever since I was a young girl, I have had a deep love for children and have always wanted to help in any way I could. I remember babysitting for my mum’s friends and family, which I loved doing. At the age of 17, I decided that this would be my life path, so I went on to college and did my CACHE Level 3 qualification in Childcare & Education. Since then, I have been working in the field of childcare and have gained lots of experience in this area. After working for a few years in London, I decided to widen my horizon, and I moved to Dubai in 2014. Since then, I have gained valuable teaching experience and leadership roles in nurseries and schools across Dubai. To enhance my knowledge and education, I completed my CACHE Level 5 in Dubai in 2020. Working in Dubai and at Best Kidz Nursery, is an environment where we provide a safe, loving, and stimulating environment that will help each child reach their fullest potential. I strive to create a positive atmosphere where children can grow and explore while learning essential life skills such as problem-solving, communication, empathy, and collaboration that every child deserves an opportunity to be loved, nurtured, and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Negar Khodabandi
Nursery Manager
