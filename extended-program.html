Curriculum

A Modern Curriculum From Finland For Early Education

THE CREATIVE POD – ART AND CRAFT ACTIVITIES

Including art as an important part of a children’s daily routine can have an enormous impact on their growth. Not only will it prepare the children for academic success in school, but it will foster early emotional development and offer them an opportunity to express themselves creatively.

Objectives and Outcomes Through art, children will:
  • Strengthen spatial reasoning and early math skills
  • Build critical language skills
  • Fine motor skills
  • Practice decision making
  • Foster Emotional Development and Regulation
  • Enhances children’s hand eye co-ordination
  • Identification of shapes, colour and texture
  • Creativity skills
