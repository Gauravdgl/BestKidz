About Us

Best Nursery And Day Care In DIFC For Preschoolers

OUR STORY

With over 20 years’ experience in the field of education in the middle east, Best Education Group has been catering to the needs of children ranging from pre-primary to grade 12 and are now extending into the field of Early Childhood Education. Being the first of its kind in the UAE, Best Kidz Nursery adopts the basic ideology and principles from Finland’s National Core Curriculum for Early Childhood Education and Care (ECEC) 2016.

OUR VISION AND VALUES

Our vision is to lay a foundation and offer the best early years education to promote children’s holistic growth, development and learning, allowing them to bloom naturally in a caring, safe, secure and stimulating environment.

  • To guide children towards ways of living that promote health, holistic growth and personal well-being in collaboration with their guardians
  • To allow children to enjoy playful interactions, learn by playing and experience the joy of learning
  • To form the basis of a healthy psychology, which continues to grow and flourish, promoting positive lifelong learning
